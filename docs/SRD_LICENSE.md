## SRD Content Used

WhispyrKeep uses the following SRD 5.2 content:

### Character Creation

- **Races**: Human, Elf (High, Wood), Dwarf (Hill, Mountain), Halfling (Lightfoot, Stout), and other SRD races
- **Classes**: Fighter, Wizard, Cleric, Rogue, and other SRD classes
- **Backgrounds**: Acolyte, Criminal, Folk Hero, Noble, Sage, Soldier

### Core Mechanics

- **Ability Scores**: Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, Charisma
- **Skills**: The 18 skills listed in the SRD
- **Saving Throws**: Ability-based saving throws
- **Armor Class**: AC calculation rules
- **Hit Points**: HP calculation and recovery
- **Conditions**: Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, etc.

### Combat

- **Initiative**: Dexterity-based initiative
- **Attack Rolls**: d20 + modifiers vs AC
- **Damage**: Weapon and spell damage
- **Critical Hits**: Natural 20 doubles dice
- **Death Saving Throws**: Three successes/failures

### Magic

- **Spell Slots**: Level-based spell slot allocation
- **Spell Casting**: Concentration, components, ranges
- **Cantrips**: At-will spells
- **Ritual Casting**: Extended casting without slots

### Equipment

- **Weapons**: Simple and martial weapons
- **Armor**: Light, medium, heavy armor
- **Adventuring Gear**: Standard equipment list

### Resting

- **Short Rest**: 1 hour, spend Hit Dice
- **Long Rest**: 8 hours, full HP recovery

## Non-SRD Content

The following content is **NOT** from the SRD and is original to WhispyrKeep or uses separate licensing:

### Original Content

- **WhispyrKeep Game System**: The turn-based LLM DM system
- **Lore Management**: Hard canon and soft lore mechanics
- **Universe Builder**: Tone sliders and homebrew system
- **Content Rating System**: G through NC-17 ratings
- **Accessibility Features**: Low-stim mode, concise recaps

### User-Generated Content

- **User Universes**: Custom worlds created by users
- **User Characters**: Individual character builds
- **Campaign Narratives**: LLM-generated and user content
- **Homebrew Rules**: User-created content overlays

WhispyrKeep does **NOT** use any Product Identity content from Wizards of the Coast. Product Identity includes but is not limited to:

- ❌ Specific campaign settings (Forgotten Realms, Eberron, etc.)
- ❌ Specific characters (Drizzt, Elminster, etc.)
- ❌ Non-SRD monsters
- ❌ Non-SRD spells
- ❌ Non-SRD magic items

## Modifications to SRD Content

WhispyrKeep modifies SRD content in the following ways:

1. **Digital Implementation**: SRD rules are implemented as software
2. **Automation**: Dice rolls and calculations are automated
3. **LLM Integration**: Rules are interpreted by language models
4. **State Management**: Game state is tracked programmatically

These modifications do not alter the underlying game mechanics.
